About me.

I am currently a postdoc at the University of Vienna, in the group of Nathanaël Berestycki. Since January 2024, I am the PI of the FWF-ESPRIT grant on "Emergent branching structures in random geometry". My research involves branching structures and their interplay with statistical mechanics, random planar maps, random permutations, SLE and Liouville quantum gravity. Here is a cv.

Before this, I did my PhD in Paris at LPSM (Sorbonne Université) under the supervision of Élie Aïdékon. The manuscript of the thesis, entitled "Multitype growth-fragmentation processes and planar excursions", can be found here.

My pronouns are he/him.
